## 1 Corinthians (id: 27403)

Paul’s First Letter to the Corinthians

Purpose
-------

To clarify some questions the Corinthian church had raised and to address a series of specific problems that had arisen in the church there

Author
------

Paul

Date
----

AD 53–56

Setting
-------

Written from Ephesus during Paul’s third missionary journey, in response to reports and questions he had received from the church in Corinth (see [1 Corinthians 1:11](https://ref.ly/1Cor1:11); [5:1](https://ref.ly/1Cor5:1); [7:1](https://ref.ly/1Cor7:1); [8:1](https://ref.ly/1Cor8:1); [12:1](https://ref.ly/1Cor12:1); [16:1](https://ref.ly/1Cor16:1))

* **Associated Passages:** 1CO 1:1
