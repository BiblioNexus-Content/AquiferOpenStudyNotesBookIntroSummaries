## Nahum (id: 27391)

The Book of Nahum

Purpose
-------

To announce God’s coming judgment on Nineveh, the capital city of the Assyrian empire, thus offering hope to the people of Judah

Author
------

Nahum

Date
----

Sometime between 663 and 612 BC

Setting
-------

The Assyrian Empire had conquered the northern kingdom of Israel, and now the southern kingdom of Judah was in danger of facing the same fate

* **Associated Passages:** NAM 1:1
