## Romans (id: 27402)

Paul’s Letter to the Romans

Purpose
-------

1. To outline the good news about Jesus
2. To address the theological and social divisions that had arisen in a church comprised of both Jewish and non\-Jewish people

Author
------

Paul

Date
----

Likely around AD 57, near the end of his third missionary journey

Setting
-------

Written (probably in Corinth) toward the end of Paul’s missionary activity, when he was making plans to visit the church in Rome on his way to Spain, after delivering a gift to the Jerusalem church

* **Associated Passages:** ROM 1:1
